Our Products

Shaktiman Tech. Tools(India) is a renowned name in the field of manufacturing cutting tools based in India. Shaktiman is the leading manufacturer and supplier of broaches, spline gauges, gear hobs, milling cutters, other cutting tools etc. We are endowed with the latest technology, and our products are manufactured under precise caliber measures upholding to the international standards. Our motto is to maintain excellent quality and entire satisfaction of our valuable customers. Our customers are the core pillars in our enterprise, and that’s why our customer service is top-notch. We customize our products according to our customer’s demands and requirements.

  • Broaches

    Do you want broaches that are effective, original, and are designed to do the job precisely? Look no further, at Shaktiman Tech. Tools we produce broaches that are made from top quality materials. Our broaches are made in several lengths, sizes, and shapes to suit our esteemed customers. Our ability to maintain consistency has made us a renowned company in the field of cutting tools manufacturing. We can manufacture broaches up to 1600 mm length.

    Inter-Spaced Broaches

    Shaktiman interspaced broach is used where two or more matching operations are required. Some teeth cut the hole while others cut the splines. We are the leading suppliers and manufacturers in this field.

    Round Broaches

    Shaktiman round broach tool produces a circular hole on the desired material with accuracy and ease. Our round broach tools are durable and non-corrosive because they are manufactured from high-quality raw materials.
    Shaktiman round broaches are offered to our customers at competitive prices.

    Straight Spline Broaches

    We manufacture durable straight spline broaches used to form splines with teeth of straight or angular lines. A thorough inspection of each straight spline broach is carried out at every stage to maintain consistency and quality.

    Involute Spline Broaches

    Shaktiman Tech. Tools have different Involute Spline Broach with a broad range of specifications to suit your needs. We are eminent in the list of Involute Spline Broach manufacturers across the region.
    Our Involute Spline Broach is specially designed to produce an internal involute profile.

    Callibration Broaches

    Our calibration broach is used to rectify deviation caused by shrinkage due to heat treatment or any other reason. We understand different customers have different needs and specifications, so we offer a variety of calibration broaches.
    Shaktiman calibration broaches are made from quality materials to provide efficient service.

    Serration Broaches

    A straight serration broach is a tool used to make straight sided V-shaped groove profile. We offer a push and pull type of serration broach that is resistant to corrosion and will serve you for an extended period.
    We offer competitive prices to our customers for straight serration broaches.

    KeyWay Broaches

    We manufacture quality keyway broach (with or without chamfer). Shaktiman keyway broach cuts keyways with ease in the bore of a gear or wheel. Keyways are available in different dimensions to meet our customer’s needs.

    Special Profile Broaches

    Our special sizing broaches are used in producing a specific internal profile in components and are largely in demand. We manufacture the special profile broaches in various specifications to meet customer’s requirements.

    Spline Guages

    The quality raw materials and latest technology we use ensures high performance of our spline gauges.

    Spline Mandrels

    We are well known as the top spline mandrel manufacturers in the region. Our spline mandrels are user-friendly, efficient, stable, and offers high performance. The spline mandrels are inspected by our experts at every stage of the manufacturing process.

  • Surface Broaches
