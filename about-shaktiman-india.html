About Us

Shaktiman Tech. Tools(India) is an accomplished and a thriving enterprise manufacturing a wide range of cutting tools such as broaches, gear hobs, milling cutters and other unique cutters. We have become top players in the field of cutting tools manufacturing based in Patiala, India, and our position has been incomparable for several years. Shaktiman Tech. Tools was established in the year 2000. Shaktiman uses the latest technology and an upgraded quality assurance system at each stage of the production process. Shaktiman offer customized tools as per the demand and blue print provided by our customers.

Shaktiman Tech. Tools(India) manufacturing process consists of quality planning, product inspection, and quality control which not only helps us meet the defined industry guidelines but also maintains quality and caliber in the final product. Shaktima Tools offer quality cutting tools made from durable materials to our customers at competitive prices. Each tool undergoes inspection and checking at every stage of the manufacturing process. Our packaging is robust, resilient, and corrosion resistant. We have been supplying our products to OEM’s and other flourishing companies in the auto parts and machine tool companies.
